INDIAN SPORTS


HOCKEY

Players: Teams have six players on the ice at once: three forwards (center, left wing, right wing), two defensemen, and one goaltender. Equipment: Players wear protective padding, helmets, skates, and use wooden or composite sticks. Goalies wear specialized heavy gear and a facial mask.

CHESS

The Board: An 8×8 checkered grid containing 64 squares alternating between light and dark. The board is placed so that each player has a light square in their bottom right-hand corner Starting Position: Each player's 16 pieces are arranged on the two closest rows. Pawns sit on the second rank (row), while the back row features rooks in the corners, knights next to them, and bishops flanking the center. The Queen always sits on her own color (White Queen on a light square, Black Queen on a dark square), with the King on the remaining adjacent square.


CRICKET

The Toss: Before the match begins, the team captains toss a coin. The winner decides whether their team will bat (attack) or field/bowl (defend) first. Batting: Two batsmen from the batting team are on the pitch at a time, standing in front of their respective wickets. One acts as the striker to hit the ball, while the other is the non-striker. Bowling and Overs: A bowler from the fielding team delivers a hard leather ball toward the striker. A delivery is called an over when the bowler completes 6 consecutive legal balls
