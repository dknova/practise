TOP SCIENTISTS

About Albert Einstein

He was a German-born theoretical physicist universally recognized as one of the greatest and most influential minds in history. He revolutionized science with his theory of relativity and mass-energy equivalence formula E = mc², and won the 1921 Nobel Prize for his explanation of the photoelectric effect.

About Issac Newton

Sir Isaac Newton was an English polymath who was a mathematician, physicist, astronomer, alchemist, theologian, author and inventor. He was a key figure in the Scientific Revolution and the Enlightenment that followed

About Charles Darwin

Charles Darwin was an English naturalist, biologist, and geologist. He is best known for his foundational theory of evolution by natural selection, which revolutionized the scientific understanding of life's diversity
